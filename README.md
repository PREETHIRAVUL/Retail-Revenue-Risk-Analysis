# Retail Revenue Risk Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales, profitability, discount impact, and revenue risk to identify key drivers of margin erosion and profit leakage. The analysis focuses on understanding how aggressive discounting, low-margin categories, and regional performance affect overall business profitability.

The project combines SQL and Power BI to uncover actionable insights and support data-driven pricing and profitability decisions.

---

## Business Problem

Despite stable revenue growth, the business experienced inconsistent profitability due to excessive discounting and low-margin product categories. Leadership lacked visibility into:

* Revenue contributing to profit loss
* Impact of discounting on margins
* High-risk categories and regions
* Opportunities for profit recovery

---

## Objective

* Analyze the relationship between discounts, revenue, and profitability
* Identify high-risk revenue segments and loss-making categories
* Evaluate regional profit concentration and recovery opportunities
* Support pricing optimization and margin recovery strategies

---

## Tools & Technologies

* SQL

---

## Analysis Performed

* Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
* Profit Margin Analysis
* Revenue-at-Risk Analysis
* Discount Leakage Analysis
* Category-wise Profitability Analysis
* Region-wise Loss Analysis
* Profit Recovery Opportunity Analysis
* KPI & Dashboard Development

---

## Key Insights

* Discounts above 30% resulted in negative profitability
* ₹0.61M revenue identified as revenue at risk due to low margins
* Office Supplies and Furniture contributed most to margin erosion
* East and Central regions showed the highest loss concentration
* ₹43.28K recoverable profit identified through pricing optimization

---

## Recommendations

* Implement discount approval thresholds
* Monitor revenue-at-risk as a business KPI
* Focus on margin efficiency instead of only revenue growth
* Prioritize corrective actions in high-loss regions and categories
* Apply targeted pricing strategies to reduce profit leakage

## Project Outcome

The analysis helped identify structural profit leakage and highlighted actionable strategies for improving profitability, optimizing pricing decisions, and reducing revenue risk.
